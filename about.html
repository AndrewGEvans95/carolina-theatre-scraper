Movie Schedule The Daily Cinema Club Why This Exists

The official Carolina Theatre website is unnecessarily complex. This site provides a clean, fast, no-nonsense listing of what's actually playing.

Powered by an automated backend script that gathers and organizes showtimes with precision. The result? A constantly up-to-date, frustration-free experience.

No fluff, no distractions—just movies and their showtimes.
